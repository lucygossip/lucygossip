About

Lucy Gossip is a London based composer, who graduated from the University of Leeds (BA Music) and Leeds College of music (MMus Creative Musician). Her music spans classical and musical theatre genres.

Recently, Lucy has composed the music for a ‘Mrs Beeton’, a musical with the book and lyrics written by Zella Compton. ‘Mrs Beeton’ was performed at Make It SO, an event at Nuffield Southampton Theatres, City, for which Lucy was the Music Director.

Lucy is the Assistant Music Director of Carpe Harmonia, a London-based amateur choir. During the coronavirus pandemic, Lucy wrote a piece for the choir, titled ‘No Matter Where You Are.’ This was performed as a virtual choir project by several Carpe Harmonia members, and released as a video.

She is currently working on various musicals. She continues to develop ‘Mrs Beeton’ and is in collaboration with lyricists on other projects.

Lucy also plays these instruments: violin, piano, voice, ukulele and flute.
